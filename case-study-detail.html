About the Company

AG Compa is a locally focused agriculture machinery and equipment provider serving farmers, contractors, and agricultural businesses. Through their e-commerce platform, they offer a wide range of agricultural machinery, spare parts, and accessories, enabling customers to research and purchase products online.

While the company had invested in building a solid website and product inventory, its digital presence lacked visibility at key buying moments — particularly in local and organic search.

The Challenge

When AG Compa partnered with White Bunnie, the business was facing a critical marketing challenge. Despite a strong offering, low visibility and ineffective demand generation were limiting growth, resulting in unreliable leads and increasing pressure on paid acquisition.

They had:
  • A good website with a large product catalog
  • Paid campaigns generating some traffic
But they lacked:
  • A predictable lead flow
  • Any Google Business Profile or local presence
  • Organic rankings for high-intent category and product keywords
  • Alignment between SEO and paid marketing efforts
Paid ads were compensating for low organic visibility, which resulted in higher CPCs and inconsistent conversions.

Our Approach

White Bunnie implemented a structured, multi-layered growth strategy designed to compound results over time rather than deliver short-term spikes.

Local Visibility Foundation

We started by building all of AG Compa's local presence from scratch. A Google Business Profile was created, verified, and fully optimized with accurate categories, service descriptions, imagery, and location signals.

Regular Google Business Profile posts were introduced to improve engagement and relevance, helping AG Compa appear in local search results for users actively looking for agriculture machinery and related services nearby.

This immediately unlocked a new, high-intent discovery channel that had previously not existed.

E-Commerce SEO Execution

Next, we focused on organic demand generation through a dedicated e-commerce SEO strategy. Instead of linking SEO to blogs, we redirected commercial intent toward product and category pages — ensuring that high-value queries landed users directly where purchasing decisions are made.

Keyword research identified gaps where competitors were ranking but AG Compa had no visibility. We then built optimized page structures, metadata, and internal linking to close those gaps systematically.

Paid + Organic Alignment

We restructured the paid campaigns to complement organic efforts — reducing spend on keywords where organic was gaining ground, and focusing budget on high-converting terms where immediate visibility mattered most. This reduced wasted ad spend and improved overall ROAS.

The Results

Within the first 90 days of the engagement, AG Compa saw measurable movement across all tracked channels. Organic traffic grew steadily, local discovery surged, and lead quality improved significantly.

The Google Business Profile, built from zero, began generating inbound calls and direction requests within the first month — providing a consistent, free lead channel that had not existed before.

Paid campaigns became more efficient as organic started covering previously expensive keywords, allowing the team to reallocate budget toward better-performing ad groups.
