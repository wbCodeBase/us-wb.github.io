What Is SEO?

Search engine optimization — and why it's the highest-ROI channel you're probably underusing

SEO is the process of making your website more visible on Google and other search engines when your customers are actively looking for what you sell. Unlike paid ads, it doesn't stop working when you stop paying. Done right, SEO compounds over time — each piece of optimized content, each earned backlink, each technical fix builds on the last.

In 2025, SEO also means showing up in Google's AI Overviews, in ChatGPT responses, and in Perplexity answers. US buyers increasingly start research in AI tools before they ever visit a vendor website. If you're not visible there, you're invisible at the moment of intent.

A strong SEO strategy has three layers: technical foundations that let search engines crawl and index your site, content that matches what your buyers are searching for, and authority signals (backlinks, citations, structured data) that tell Google you're trustworthy. White Bunnie manages all three.

FAQ

Common questions before hiring an SEO agency

How much do SEO services cost in the US?+
SEO services typically range from $1,500 to $10,000+ per month in the US depending on your industry, competition, and what's included. Small business local SEO programs sit at the lower end. National campaigns or competitive B2B verticals cost more. We always provide a free audit first so you understand the scope before any commitment — and we never recommend more than your situation calls for.
Do you require long-term contracts?+
No. White Bunnie operates month-to-month. We don't lock clients in because we don't need to — the results keep clients working with us. If the work isn't delivering, you should be free to leave. That accountability keeps us sharp.
How long does SEO take to show results?+
Most businesses see measurable movement in traffic and rankings within 90 to 120 days. Competitive page-one positions take 6 to 12 months depending on your starting authority and the competition level. We track leading indicators — crawl health, indexed pages, featured snippet wins — from week one so you're never waiting blind on lagging metrics. SEO compounds over time. It's not a sprint.
What is the difference between an SEO agency and an SEO company?+
The terms are used interchangeably in the US market. What matters more than the label: do they specialize in your business type, can they show verifiable results, do they measure outcomes tied to revenue rather than just rankings, and are they honest about timelines and what they can control? Those are the questions worth asking.
What is AEO and GEO — and does my business need it?+
AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures your content to appear in Google's featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and AI Overviews. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) goes further — it positions your brand to be cited or recommended inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. If your buyers use Google or any AI tool to research before buying, you need both. Most of your competitors don't have either yet — which is exactly why now is the right time to build it.
Do you work with small businesses or only B2B companies?+
Both. Small businesses and local businesses are a core part of what we do — home services, legal practices, contractors, retail, and local professional services. Our small business programs focus on local visibility, Google Business Profile, and near-me keyword strategy without requiring enterprise-level budgets. We're direct about what's realistic for each situation and don't oversell scope that a business doesn't need.
