Self-Assessment

Score your website
in 5 minutes

One point per yes. 20 points total. Find out your AEO level right now -before you request the professional audit.

Content

5 questions
  • You have pages whose sole purpose is answering a specific buyer question -not a service page with a FAQ section bolted on
  • Your answers are direct and declarative -the answer comes first, the supporting context follows
  • You have content addressing comparison queries: "X vs Y," "alternatives to X," "best X for [vertical]"
  • You cover the full buyer journey: awareness, evaluation, and decision-stage questions are all addressed
  • You publish original frameworks, data, or research that others in your niche would cite

Technical

5 questions
  • FAQ schema is deployed and validates in Google's Rich Results Test
  • Organisation schema is present with complete attributes including sameAs links to LinkedIn, Crunchbase & directories
  • Service schema is live on all service pages with accurate type, provider & area attributes
  • Your site scores 90+ on Core Web Vitals for both mobile and desktop
  • Key pages have no important content trapped in JavaScript rendering or images

Authority

5 questions
  • Every piece of content has a named author with verifiable, relevant credentials
  • Your brand has been mentioned in at least 3 niche B2B tech publications in the past 12 months
  • You have a non-promotional presence in Reddit communities where your buyers discuss vendors
  • Your company is accurately described in at least 5 entity sources: LinkedIn, Crunchbase, G2, Clutch, and one industry directory
  • At least one piece of your content has been cited by a third party without you asking for it

Measurement

5 questions
  • You have tested your top 10 buyer queries in ChatGPT, Perplexity & Google AI in the past 30 days
  • You track AI-sourced referral traffic in GA4 (from ChatGPT.com, Perplexity.ai, etc.)
  • You know which competitors are currently cited more often than you across AI platforms
  • You monitor brand citation frequency monthly across a defined set of target queries
  • You have a process for verifying and correcting what AI says about your company
